Tea

Description

Tea is a hot drink famous in nepal and many eastern countries of Asia. It starts the day for many people in nepal.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tea leaves
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Pour water in kettle.
  2. Add tea leaves in the kettle with water.
  3. Boil it for 2-4 minutes.
  4. Add milk and sugar, according to your liking.
  5. pour it in the cup to drink it
