Chowmein

Description

Chowmein is a delicious stir-fried noodle dish loved across Nepal. It is made with noodles, vegetables, and optional meat, seasoned with soy sauce and spices.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Carrot
  • Cabbage
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil noodles in hot water until soft, then drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions and garlic.
  3. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  4. Add the noodles and mix well with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  5. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until evenly mixed.
  6. Serve hot with ketchup or chili sauce.
