Momo

Description

Momo is a popular Nepali dish inspired by Tibetan dumplings. It is filled with minced meat or vegetables and steamed to perfection. It is often served with a spicy tomato chutney.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Minced meat or vegetables
  • Onion
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour with water and a pinch of salt to make dough.
  2. Prepare the filling using minced meat or vegetables, onion, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Roll the dough into small circles and add a spoonful of filling.
  4. Fold and seal the edges to form momos.

  6. Serve hot with spicy chutney.
